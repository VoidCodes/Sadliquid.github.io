My Achievements

I participated in the Singapore Games Creation Competition in 2019 and supported our other team in the Semi-Finals.
We used Construct-3 to design our game and this gave me insight on how I can utilise my skills in IT in the real world.
Although we didn't make it into the finals, it was a valuble experience to be able to compete against like-minded individuals and have fun at the same time.

I helped my team design the robot submission for the National Robotics Competition in 2019.
Our task was to design a robot to pick up of various colored bricks and place them onto specific places on the playmat.
We didn't make it to the runner-ups either, but it sure was an exciting event!


I achieved Gold in the Secondary 3 Badminton Inter-Class Games with my team.
We started off on a losing end but with constant perseverance and teamwork, we managed to arise victorious as first place in the cohort!


I also achieved certificates for completing Python and SQL modules too!
Learning something aligned with my interests during my own free time definitely was fun and enjoyable!
